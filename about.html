We are a leading Saudi esports club established in 2021 and licensed by the Saudi Federation of Esports.We are a small part of the rapidly growing es- ports community that is driven by the desire and ambition for greater exclu- sivity .

FOUNDED IN 2015

The club was co-founded by legendary League of Legends player-turned-team owner, Carlos ‘Ocelote’ Rodriguez, and veteran esports entrepreneur and investor, Jens Hilgers.

With decades of experience, G2’s multinational staff elevates the company to the highest levels of performance in every aspect of business in the esports industry.
